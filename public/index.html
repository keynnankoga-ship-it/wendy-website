I’ve been thinking about how to say this without sounding like a malfunctioning romantic comedy character… but unfortunately that ship has already sailed, crashed into the harbor, and is now on fire.

This website is dedicated to a friend I met on Snapchat—someone who started as just a random connection but slowly became an important part of my life.

Even though we didn’t meet in the usual way, our conversations, shared moments, and the time we’ve spent getting to know each other have meant a lot to me. It’s funny how someone you’ve never met in person can still make your day better, make you laugh, and be there when you need it.

This dedication is a small way of appreciating that connection. It may not be a traditional friendship, but it’s real in its own way, and it matters.
